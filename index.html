Platform Highlights

Why CaseFlow Exists

SMEs lose billions annually to invoice disputes managed through emails and spreadsheets. CaseFlow brings structure, evidence, and auditability to commercial dispute resolution.

Evidence Ingestion

Upload invoices, POs, contracts, delivery notes, email exports and photos. Structured capture with completeness checks ensures nothing is missed.

Root-Cause Taxonomy

Standard reason codes map each dispute to required evidence checklists and resolution playbooks price mismatch, quantity variance, quality claims and more.

Timeline Builder

Chronological single version of events from contract through to dispute notice. Automatically flags missing artefacts and contradictory data points.

Evidence Sufficiency Scoring

Quantifiable completeness assessment detects missing documentation or discrepancies measuring proof quality, not predicting legal outcomes.

Resolution Package Generator

Structured pack including factual dispute summary, versioned evidence index, quantified commercial options, and template communications outputs, not legal advice.

Legal-Safe by Construction

Templates separate facts from interpretation, disallow advisory language, and include built-in disclaimers allowing non-legal staff to create professional documentation safely.
